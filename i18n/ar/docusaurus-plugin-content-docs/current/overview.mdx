Shira is an anti-phishing training tool. You can use Shira in your team or organization to train your colleagues on identifying phishing attacks sent through email, SMS, and messaging apps.

Shira focuses on recreating, in a controlled learning environment, phishing simulations that are as similar as possible to what your team will be receiving in real life. 

Shira is built around the recent research findings that show that when it comes to anti-phishing skill building, [education beats simulation](/phishing-quizzes).

In this help center you can find information and guidance on how to use Shira.

## Ready-made quizzes {#ready-made-quizzes}
Our [ready-made quizzes](/ready-made) let users take a 10-question quiz that is customized to look as similar as possible to what they will experience in real life. It's free and accessible to anyone.

## Shira Space {#shira-space}
A [Shira Space](/spaces) offers advanced features for teams and organizations, like:
- creating your own quiz questions, with content specifically relevant to your team.
- setting your quizz's privacy as public (accessible to anyone with the quiz URL) or private (only accessible to learners registered with your organization). Private quizzes are coming soon!
- tracking user Results to understand where your users can improve.

[Learn more about Shira spaces](/spaces) or if you're already convinced, [create your space](/contact). You can always [contact us if you need help](/contact).

export function FeaturedDocs() {
  const category = useCurrentSidebarCategory();

    (item) =>

      item.items?.some(

      )
  ).items;

    <div className={classNames(global.shiraDocCards)}>
      {spacesItems.map((item, idx) => (

  );
